### Detailed Description:

#### Diagram 1:
- Two particles enter a uniform magnetic field \( B \) directed into the page.
- The magnetic field lines are represented by crosses (\( \times \)).
- The initial velocity \( v_0 \) is perpendicular to the magnetic field.
- Paths of the particles are labeled 1, 2, 3, and 4.

#### Table:
- Particle \( Y \): Charge \( -Q \), Mass \( M \)
- Particle \( Z \): Charge \( +Q \), Mass \( 2M \)

#### Diagram 2:
- Axes: \( x \), \( y \), and \( z \) (positive \( z \) out of the page).
- Electric field \( E \) in the +x-direction.
- Magnetic field \( B \) in the +y-direction.

---

### Solution:

#### Problem 7:
To determine the paths of the particles, we need to consider the Lorentz force acting on each particle due to the magnetic field. The force is given by \( \vec{F} = q(\vec{v} \times \vec{B}) \).

- For particle \( Y \) with charge \( -Q \) and mass \( M \):
  - The force will be in the opposite direction to the cross product \( \vec{v} \times \vec{B} \).
  - Since \( \vec{v} \) is perpendicular to \( \vec{B} \) and into the page, the force will be downward.
  - Therefore, particle \( Y \) will follow path 4.

- For particle \( Z \) with charge \( +Q \) and mass \( 2M \):
  - The force will be in the direction of the cross product \( \vec{v} \times \vec{B} \).
  - Since \( \vec{v} \) is perpendicular to \( \vec{B} \) and into the page, the force will be upward.
  - Therefore, particle \( Z \) will follow path 1.

Thus, the correct answer is (D) 4 1.

#### Problem 8:
For a positively charged particle to pass through both the electric and magnetic fields in a straight line at a constant speed, the electric force must balance the magnetic force. The electric force is given by \( \vec{F}_E = q\vec{E} \) and the magnetic force is given by \( \vec{F}_B = q(\vec{v} \times \vec{B}) \).

- The electric field \( E \) is in the +x-direction.
- The magnetic field \( B \) is in the +y-direction.
- For the forces to balance, the velocity \( \vec{v} \) must be in the +z-direction.

Thus, the correct answer is (D) +z.
