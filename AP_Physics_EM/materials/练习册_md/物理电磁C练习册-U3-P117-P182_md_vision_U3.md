### Detailed Description of the Circuit Diagram:
- A 120V battery is connected in series with three resistors: \( R_1 = 10 \Omega \), \( R_2 = 20 \Omega \), and \( R_3 \).
- An ammeter measures the total current \( I = 6A \) flowing through the circuit.
- The resistors are connected in series, meaning the same current flows through each resistor.

### Solution for Question 28:
To find the resistance of \( R_3 \):

1. **Total Voltage (V):** 120V
2. **Total Current (I):** 6A
3. **Total Resistance (R_total):** Using Ohm's Law, \( R_{\text{total}} = \frac{V}{I} = \frac{120V}{6A} = 20 \Omega \)

Since the resistors are in series, the total resistance is the sum of the individual resistances:
\[ R_{\text{total}} = R_1 + R_2 + R_3 \]
\[ 20 \Omega = 10 \Omega + 20 \Omega + R_3 \]
\[ R_3 = 20 \Omega - 30 \Omega = -10 \Omega \]

However, this result is not physically possible since resistance cannot be negative. Therefore, there might be an error in the problem statement or the provided values. Given the options, the closest reasonable value is:

**Answer: B. 10 Ω**

This suggests that there might be a mistake in the problem setup or the provided values, but based on the given options, 10 Ω is the most plausible answer.

### 解释与推理

题目描述了一个由理想电池、开关、电容器和电阻器组成的串联电路。当开关闭合时，电流开始流动，电容器开始充电。我们需要找到电流减半所需的时间 \( t \)。

在RC电路中，电流随时间的变化可以用以下公式表示：
\[ I(t) = I_0 e^{-\frac{t}{RC}} \]
其中 \( I_0 \) 是初始电流，\( R \) 是电阻，\( C \) 是电容，\( t \) 是时间。

当电流减半时，我们有：
\[ I(t) = \frac{I_0}{2} \]

将这个条件代入上述公式：
\[ \frac{I_0}{2} = I_0 e^{-\frac{t}{RC}} \]

两边同时除以 \( I_0 \)：
\[ \frac{1}{2} = e^{-\frac{t}{RC}} \]

取自然对数：
\[ \ln\left(\frac{1}{2}\right) = -\frac{t}{RC} \]

由于 \( \ln\left(\frac{1}{2}\right) = -\ln(2) \)：
\[ -\ln(2) = -\frac{t}{RC} \]

两边同时乘以 -1：
\[ \ln(2) = \frac{t}{RC} \]

因此：
\[ t = RC \ln(2) \]

已知 \( \ln(2) \approx 0.693 \)，所以：
\[ t \approx 0.693RC \]

对比选项，最接近的值是：
- D. \( t = 0.69RC \)

### 最终答案
**D. \( t = 0.69RC \)**
